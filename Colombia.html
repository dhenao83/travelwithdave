Hello to all! Ever think about taking trip some place far and abroad but you are stressing over how to plan it and where to stay and what sites to visit? Well stress no more! Here is some great info on all of those things! Enjoy!

This hostel in Colombia is really awesome, nice and clean! There are locations in the cities of Bogota and Cartagena. I recommend this hostel because it is close to attractions, transportation and great restaurants! Click the pic and check it out!

CasaKiwi Hostel is located in Medellin, Colombia and is a great way to meet new people and experience the city! It is conveniently located and easily affordable. Try their in-house empanadas! Delicious!

The Dreamer Hostel is located in Santa Marta along the coast of Colombia. It is a must go to place for beach lovers and those who want some R and R. Also the fruit and fruit juices here are the best I have ever had in my life! Be sure to try one!

Don't dare miss visiting La Candelaria in Bogota, Colombia. For all of you art and history lovers this is the place to be and to experience the warmth and friendliness of its natives and breathe the wonderfully clean air of the Andes mountains.

Santa Marta is a must-see place for all those who love drinking margaritas on the beach and just relaxing with friends, visiting parks, taking hikes and seeing wildlife.

Boyaca is about a two and a half hour drive outside of Bogota and is well worth the travel effort. Check out one of the most beautiful Cathedral and town that Colombia has to offer.
