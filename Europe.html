Hello to all! Ever think about taking trip some place far and abroad but you are stressing over how to plan it and where to stay and what sites to visit? Well stress no more! Here is some great info on all of those things! Enjoy!

Antoni Gaudi's "Sagrada Familia" Cathedral in Barcelona, Spain is quite possibly the most magnificent Cathedral that I have been to in my life and I have been to many! Inside and out you will discover Gaudi's very distinguished and peculiar architectural style which is exactly why it is genius!

Italy is a wonderful place to visit for many reasons and there are so many great cities and countrysides to explore; However, my absolute favorite is Venice, Italy. Venice is a unique city connected by bridges and canals. Be sure to navigate this great, unique place by taking a gondola ride!

The Eiffel Tower is probably my absolute favorite structure to visit in Europe. Although it is a pretty stereotypical touristy thing to do, it is still an amazing site to see and a beautifully shaped architectural complex. You can go up the tower to view the city or just simply lay on the park grass in front of it to enjoy the view.

If you are looking to go to Paris, here is a great selection of hotels that you will be able to afford and best of all they are all within walking distance of all sites in Paris including the Eiffel Tower, Champs-Elysee and more!

Stonehenge is one of the most amazingly built structures on Earth! It is also one of the greatest mysteries because it is argued how it was made or even why. It is a magnificent site to see, located in Salisbury, England just a couple of hours away from London.

The Eurail is in my opinion the best way to see all of Europe. The Eurail covers almost all of Western Europe but not completely all of Eastern Europe, so be sure to check the site of what specific countries the rail covers. Happy riding!
