Hello to all! Ever think about taking trip some place far and abroad but you are stressing over how to plan it and where to stay and what sites to visit? Well stress no more! Here is some great info on all of those things! Enjoy!

Melbourne is a great city packed with plenty of things to do, see and has a beach right along the coast! It is a very friendly and clean place with great weather all year round! Here is a list of decent, reasonably priced hostels.

Don't leave Australia without first checking out the Sydney Opera House and walking up the Sydney Harbour Bridge. There is a guided walking tour provided to climb up to the top of the bridge, have a drink and back down, it's good fun!

The Great Barrier Reef is an amazing site to see for those of you who are comfortable with diving under at least 30 feet of water. It is the largest living coral reef on the planet and also very pretty! The reef is located in Cairns which is in North Queensland of Australia and in addition to hosting the reef, is also a cute little beachtown!

The Great Ocean Road is located in Geelong just SouthEast of Melbourne. I promise you it will be one of the most amazing sites that you ever see in your life! If you get a chance, check out one of the tours offered or better yet rent a car and drive the road yourself!

Visiting the Australian Outback is probably one of the wildest thigns that you can do while in Australia. There are many guided tours but many have also been known to rent a camper or vehicle and do it themselves. Do you dare venture into the Outback?!

For all you surfers out there, Australia's Gold Coast is a surfer's dream! The Gold Coast is not just for surfer's it is a major city in Australia and it is popular for many water related activities. The nightlife in the Gold Coast is definitely a reason to party for also!
