Hello to all! Ever think about taking trip some place far and abroad but you are stressing over how to plan it and where to stay and what sites to visit? Well stress no more! Here is some great info on all of those things! Enjoy!

China is so humongous, ethnically homogenous and heavily populated countries on the planet. With a population of 1.3 billion inhabitants, China continues to grow as well as its economy. There are so many amazing and great sites to see in China; from deserts to cities to grasslands it should really be considered a continent!

Colombia is a beautiful country with diverse landscapes, plenty of forests and camping sites, beaches, the beautiful Andes Mountains, a city packed with Native Indian and European history and a growing art scene with progressively improving development and lifestyle that it is attracting a wide diversity of tourists now from all over the world.

Europe holds the most diverse amount of countries probably next to Africa. The amount of different cultures and laguages spoken here will amaze you along with the amount of history, art and museums and sites there are to see. Discover probably one of the most efficient railway systems on the planet, the Eurail which has the ability to connect you to any country that you want to visit all the way from Western Europe to Eastern Europe and nearby Russia!

Australia is another country with a huge landscape and quite possibly one of the most diverse landscapes on the planet! Australia's terrain consists of deserts, mountains, beaches, city, the outback and more! Some of Australia's major cities include Melbourne, Sydney, Perth, Adelaide, Cairns where the Great Barrier Reef is located, and Darwin in the North near the Outback. Australia is also known for its great amount of outdoor activities so you can indulge whether you are a swimmer, hiker, mountain climber,skydiver, waterskier, you name it!
